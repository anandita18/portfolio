About

I am a curious, self driven and passionate learner hoping to one day call myself a UX Researcher & Designer. I have a Bachelors degree in Supply Chain Analytics and Management Information Systems from Purdue University, West Lafayette IN. My problem-solving technique is to find points of tension behind a situation and do several iterations on each point. I believe that access to information and technology can create an inclusive and equal world. Aside from this, I like to increase my cultural intelligence through traveling, and documenting my travels. I am a non-fiction enthusiast, and a podcast fanatic. Some of my recent favorite authors include Dan Ariely and Malcolm Gladwell. I also enjoy watching documentaries on conspiracy theories, and TED talks for my daily dose of inspiration.

Thanks for stopping by!
