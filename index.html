These are some of the UX Projects I have worked on

For this project, our client was Office of Global Michigan that supports immigrant and refugee integration initiatives for the state. There are voids in the information that the Office of Global Michigan is collecting on refugee services. In order to provide better support, they need to find out how refugees are accessing mainstream services and the challenges they face when navigating the systems that host these services.

Receipts are a commonly overlooked part of a customer’s shopping experience, and often end up lost or in the garbage. This makes returns, exchanges, and any other post transaction interactions more difficult for both the business and the consumers. This project provides a solution to make Apple Pay more intuitive and seamless for both the consumer and the business by essentially eliminating paper receipts.

Travelling and navigating through an unfamiliar city solo wasn’t easy. Seeing multiple crime alerts, and reading horror stories online made it difficult to predict which areas to avoid. Therefore, I decided to explore the area of travel safety. I developed a mobile prototype using the fundamental readings about the design process and best practices.
